Ratatouille

Description

Ratatouille is a French dish consisting of eggplant, tomatoes, green peppers, squash, and sometimes meat.

Ingredients

  • tomatoes
  • eggplant
  • squash
  • marinara sauce
  • onion
  • olive oil
  • fresh parsley
  • dried thyme
  • basil
  • garlic

Directions

  1. Pour marinara sauce into a circular pan.
  2. Slice the eggplant, squash, and tomatoe until you have several dozens of slices of approximately the same size.
  3. Assemble the vegetable slices in the pan as seen in the picture above.
  4. In a separate pan, cook some olive oil, garlic, fresh parsley, dried thyme, and dried basil.
  5. Drizzle the olive oil mixture across the vegetable slices.
  6. Cook in the oven for 40 minutes covered with foil, then 20 additional minutes without the foil.
