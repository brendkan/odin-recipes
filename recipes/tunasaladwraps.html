Tuna Salad Wrap

Description

Make tuna salad and then wrap it in lettuce. 10/10.

Ingredients

  • tuna
  • mayonnaise
  • mustard
  • cornichon pickles
  • eggs
  • celery
  • onion
  • head of lettuce

Directions

  1. Chop up celery, place in a bowl, and salt thoroughly. The salt will draw water out, making it easier to squeeze out the liquid later.
  2. Put tuna, mayonnaise, mustard, and your minced cornichon pickles in a large bowl, and mix.
  3. Boil some eggs, then slice them and add them to the mix.
  4. Squeeze as much water out of the celery as possible, then add to the mix.
  5. Mix well and eat with lettuce wraps.
